About Our College

Welcome to TechVista Institute of Engineering, a leading institution in engineering education and innovation.

Established in 1963, our college has consistently strived for excellence in academic and research pursuits. Our mission is to nurture talented individuals, equip them with the skills and knowledge required for the ever-evolving engineering landscape, and instill a passion for innovation.

Why Choose TechVista?

  • State-of-the-art laboratories and facilities that enable hands-on learning experiences.
  • Experienced and dedicated faculty members who are experts in their fields.
  • Collaborative research opportunities with industry partners and research institutions.
  • Wide range of engineering disciplines to choose from, catering to diverse interests.
  • Strong emphasis on holistic development through extracurricular activities and clubs.
  • Supportive and inclusive learning environment that promotes innovation and creativity.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in engineering education, research, and innovation. We aim to produce skilled engineers who are not only technically proficient but also socially responsible and ethical contributors to society.

Core Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Innovation and Research
  • Ethical and Moral Values
  • Diversity and Inclusivity
  • Continuous Learning
  • Collaboration and Teamwork

Join us in our pursuit of knowledge and excellence. Discover your potential and make your mark in the world of engineering at TechVista.

Departments

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)

Empowering future tech leaders, our CSE department blends theoretical depth with hands-on coding, fostering innovation in software development and AI applications.

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

At the forefront of communication technology, our ECE department equips graduates with expertise in wireless systems, signal processing, and advanced electronics.

Mechanical Engineering

Pioneering sustainable solutions, our Mechanical Engineering department combines theory with practical design, preparing students for dynamic roles in energy, robotics, and manufacturing.

Applied Information and Data Science (AIDS)

From big data to actionable insights, the AIDS department empowers students with the skills to analyze complex data, making informed decisions in diverse fields.

Civil and Construction Engineering (CCE)

Building the future with precision, our CCE department focuses on sustainable infrastructure design and management, shaping urban landscapes and enhancing communities.

Admissions

Welcome to our admissions process! We are excited to have you join our engineering college. Here's what you need to know:

Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to identify passionate and dedicated students who are ready to excel in the field of engineering. It involves:

  • Submission of online application form
  • Entrance exam or merit-based selection
  • Personal interview
  • Final selection and offer letter

Cutoff Marks

The cutoff marks for admission vary based on the department and course. We take into consideration your academic performance, entrance exam scores, and other relevant factors.

To check the specific cutoff marks for each course and department, please visit our admissions portal.

Scholarships

Merit Scholarship

Our Merit Scholarship is awarded to students with exceptional academic achievements. It covers a percentage of tuition fees based on the student's academic performance.

Eligibility: Top 10% of the incoming students

Application Deadline: May 31, 2023

Need-Based Scholarship

The Need-Based Scholarship supports students with financial need. Eligibility is determined based on a comprehensive assessment of the student's financial situation.

Eligibility: Demonstrated financial need

Application Deadline: April 15, 2023

Sports Excellence Scholarship

We recognize outstanding achievements in sports. The Sports Excellence Scholarship is awarded to students who have excelled in sports at the national or state level.

Eligibility: National or state level sports achievements

Application Deadline: July 20, 2023
